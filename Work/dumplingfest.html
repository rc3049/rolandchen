Dumpling Fest

2023, Hancock New York

Dumpling Fest was a month long residency at unclebrother restaurant culminating in a cyborging, dumpling-based weekend restaurant takeover.

,

The menu items were thought about as media products. For example, I made "long" and "short" sauce. They were separated by time but in terms of taste profile.
