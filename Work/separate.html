Separate, Distribute, Spread, Execute, Inherit, Align

2025, Installation/Performance, 2.5 hours, Oxford, UK

What do dumpling making, manufacturing practices, and human connection have in common? More than you think.

I’m excited to invite you to “Wrap and Fold Affective Solutions” a participatory performance to explore scalable solutions to drive dumpling growth and intimacy efficiency. We will curate an intimate dinner gatherings to foster meaningful connections and idea exchange. Together, we’ll make dumplings, share stories, and reflect on the ways food, care, and work intersect in our daily lives—one fold at a time.

Let’s punch in, punch out, knead, fold, and create together—because sometimes, the most meaningful connections are made with our hands.

#PerformanceArt #CulturalPreservation #LaborOfLove #DumplingMaking #ManufacturingIntimacy #InformalEconomies
