Not Too Sweet

2023, Performance, Seyðisfjörður, Iceland

"Reverse mukbang" using overly sweet store-bought chinese spice mix. Tracy uses a typical mukbang script, while I read from "Fortress Besieged" from Qian Zhongshu. Streamed to Instagram live.

The installation includes a family recipe for red bean soup projected on the wall while viewers are enveloped in pink light and a scent diffuser. The goal is to complicate the viewer’s expectations of Asian food and bodies as passive, soft, or sweet.

A spoken word monologue and ambient music fill the space, drawing from romantic tropes in Chinese pop music and the speaker's relationship to his own cultural memory.
