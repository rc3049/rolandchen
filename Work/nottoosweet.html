Not Too Sweet

2023, Performance, Seyðisfjörður, Iceland

"Reverse mukbang" using overly sweet store-bought chinese spice mix. Tracy uses a typical mukbang script, while I read from "Fortress Besieged" from 钱钟书. Streamed to Instagram live.

Pictures of Jamie Oliver making and eating "Asian" food lined the walls of the gallery space as you walked in.
